The ABC School

The ABC School, started Its journey on April 10th 2015 and is growing ever since. The school is located in the prestigious sector of Bareilly. The Abc is well equipped and geared for providing the highest standards of education through well researched teaching methodologies and an excellent teacher-student rapport and ratio. We believe that education is not a medium of growing rich and learned . it is a medium which helps us to deepen our insight, creating a meaningful outlook and widening our horizon. The core focus on education is its holistic development and that’s what we believe in.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.
